About 8-Bit Software





  • 8-Bit Software (8BS) is a postal user group
    supporting Acorn BBC and Master computers.



  • 8BS is non profit making, I run the group
    in my spare time.



  • 8BS has a Public Domain library of 1000 discs
    of free software



  • 8BS features a regular disc based magazine.
    Here are some articles from it. Here are some games
    reviews     from it



  • 8BS has in stock lots
    of second hand spares, books and software



  • 8BS supports PC users with BBC emulators



  • 8BS has a wealth of knowledge amongst its
    ranks. If I don't know the answer, I know someone who does!
