Spare Master Cartridge news
===========================
As promised some time ago I have been looking into the possibility of
restarting fabrication of Master cartridges.

Having phoned round several PCB manufacturers (since this is where the
majority of the expenses go) I have found that these could be made at a cost
of `9.50 each.

Note that these do not include the beige plastic cases though.

The boards would be double sided,ready assembled and checked.Each with a unique
id number and 1 year warranty.

Now for the bad news - the minimum order quantity from the PCB manufacturer
is 50 boards.Some simple maths reveals that this is about `500 outlay for me,
which I can't afford.So,orders must be accompanied by a `5 per board deposit.

Please indicate AS SOON AS POSSIBLE how many you all might be interested in
buying.If the result is less than 50 then sorry,but it wont go ahead.If more
than 50 boards are needed then I shall request the deposits from you and
send for the boards.I have been quoted 14-16 days for them to be made.
